Our mission at Careers in Calculation is to empower young people to make informed financial decisions by providing comprehensive education and practical tools. We strive to instill financial literacy and understanding of personal finance through interactive income and checkbook calculators. Our goal is to equip the next generation with the skills and knowledge necessary to achieve financial stability and success in their careers.

This project was created by Gabrielle Ruiz-Yeager at the Center for Advanced Research and Technology.
